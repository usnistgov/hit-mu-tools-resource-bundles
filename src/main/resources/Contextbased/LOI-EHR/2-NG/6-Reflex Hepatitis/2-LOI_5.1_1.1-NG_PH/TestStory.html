Description
A female prisoner from the local correctional facility is seen at the Good Health Clinic By Dr Radon. She has a history of substance abuse is tested for Hepatitis A, B and C. Dr Radon orders a hepatitis panel from Century Hospital Clinical Laboratory. The order is transmitted electronically through the clinic's EHR system using the LOI message. The serum specimen is collected by the Good Health Clinic Staff and sent to the hospital lab for testing.
