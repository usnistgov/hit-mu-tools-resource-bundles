Description
Geronimo Alto, a 78 year old patient, is seen by Dr. Ramirez at the Los Angeles Clinic for a prostate biopsy, due to trouble urinating to rule out Prostate Cancer. Dr. Ramirez collects 10 biopsy samples via a transrectal needle biopsy procedure, places them into their respective containers with 10% Formalin and sends them off immediately to the clinical lab at Century Hospital.
PreCondition
No Pre Condition
PostCondition
No Post Condition

  • Minimally populated MSH Segment - Demonstrate the capability to support only minimally required data elements in the MSH segment.
  • Minimally populated ORC Segment - Demonstrate the capability to support only minimally required data elements in the ORC segment.
  • Minimally populated OBR Segment - Demonstrate the capability to support only minimally required data elements in the OBR segment.
  • Minimally populated DG1 Segment - Demonstrate the capability to support only minimally required data elements in the DG1 segment.
  • Minimally populated SPM Segment - Demonstrate the capability to support only minimally required data elements in the SPM segment.
