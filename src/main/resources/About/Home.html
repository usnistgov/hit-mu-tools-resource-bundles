Supporting

The NIST Syndromic Surveillance Test Suite supports the testing of HL7 v2.5.1 messages in support of the Syndromic Surveillance Community.

  • HL7 V2.5.1 PHIN Messaging Guide Release 2.0 and associated Erratum
  • 2015 ONC Health IT Certification
2015 ONC Health IT Certification Start ONC Testing

The NIST Syndromic Surveillance Test Suite is the test tool used for certifying ONC 2015 Edition Health IT Modules. The capability used for ONC Certification can be accessed by navigating to the ONC 2015 Certification option found on the HL7 Context-based tab. Have a Question?

A Google Group (HL7v2 Syndromic Testing) has been established for discussion/questions about the Implementation Guide, testing tool, and testing issues. No membership is required. A Google account is required for posting. Note: The same Google Group is being used for submitting questions/comments related to the 2014 and 2015 Editions of the NIST Certification Test Tool and general Syndromic Surveillance related topics. When asking questions, please specify the edition of the tool and version of the standard to which the questions apply.

  • Site: http://groups.google.com/d/forum/hl7v2-syndromic-testing
  • Email: hl7v2-immunization-testing@googlegroups.com
